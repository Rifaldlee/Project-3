Processor

is the main component or brain of your laptop/computer. The processor is located right in the middle of the motherboard. The processor has an important function for all computer device operations because all commands start from the processor. So Therefore, choosing the best processor to support work using a computer or laptop is very important for users. Currently, for computer processors, there are 2 competitors, namely AMD and Intel. Intel processors have the advantage in power efficiency. while the AMD processor excels with multithreading and multitasking

Motherboard

The motherboard is a circuit board that is inside a computer case where all the computer components are connected. The motherboard provides connectivity between computer hardware components, such as the processor (CPU), memory (RAM), hard disk drives, and video cards. The motherboard itself also comes in a variety of different types and sizes, you know, for example, the Intel type processor has a series motherboard type (Zxxx, Hxxx, Bxxx) then the AMD processor has a motherboard type. series (Axx,Bxx,Xxx)

VGA cards

VGA is a Video Graphics Accelerator that has a function as a way to process graphic data to be able to display the monitor screen. In addition, the VGA Card also has a processor section which is usually called a GPU or Graphics Processing Unit and requires a certain amount of memory. On the market there are 2 brands that compete with each other in the vga card sales arena, namely AMD and Nvidia. Generally, the level of a VGA card is calculated from the number behind it, the bigger the number. the back, the better and more expensive

Power supply

is one of the hardware components that acts as a provider of electricity and power used to power computers and other devices. The power supply receives energy from an electrical outlet and converts AC current (alternating current) to DC (direct current) energy that a computer needs. This power supply, which is sometimes abbreviated as PS, P/S, or PSU, also regulates the voltage and flow to the device to prevent overheating or overheating. on power a good supply there is a sequence of efficiency categories such as (80+ bronze, silver, gold, platinum and titanium)

Storage

Storage is a digital device that serves to store various kinds of digital data. which can be stored for an indefinite period of time depending on the age and maintenance of the Storage device itself. Currently, Storage devices continue to be developed both in terms of device types, and innovations in size. Because the Storage device over time has a smaller size. Examples of storage on a computer are ssd, hard disk, M.2 ssd, M.2 NVME. The faster the reading ability, the more expensive the price

Ram

Ram is a temporary storage area on a computer whose contents can be accessed in a fixed time regardless of the location of the data in memory or random. so this Ram is only as temporary storage when you use it specific computer or software. so that it can be processed quickly. Until now the development of RAM has reached 5 generations, namely DDR1, DDR2, DDR3, DDR4 and DDR5.
